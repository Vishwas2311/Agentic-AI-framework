How the System Thinks

How Genesis distills the app

The system does not jump from files to code. It collects evidence, resolves truth, and then writes one blueprint all builder agents can trust.

A simpler story helps users understand why the pipeline is reliable.
1. Collect evidence

Genesis reads packages, videos, docs, websites, schemas, configs, and runtime traces together.

2. Resolve truth

Signals are compared, confidence is assigned, and conflicts are surfaced before generation begins.

3. Generate blueprint

One canonical app spec drives UI, backend, database, tests, docs, deployment, and replay.
